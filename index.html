About me

I'm a product manager with a background in computer science and an MBA from IIM Indore — which means I'm equally comfortable in a system design whiteboard session and a business case review.

Most of my career has been in fintech: lending infrastructure, payment rails, digital savings, and marketplace platforms. I've shipped products used by hundreds of thousands of borrowers, worked across lender integrations and credit underwriting logic, and learned that the difference between a good PM and a great one is how clearly you can see the gap between what users do and what they need.

My sweet spot is the systems layer of product — working closely with engineering on API design, data pipelines, and financial infrastructure where product decisions have long leverage.

Outside of work, I build things: side projects, tools, whatever seems interesting. Currently: an AI-powered credit card discovery platform for Indian users.
